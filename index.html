Welcome to Andreas Sellstone’s Website#

This is my personal website where you can find information about me and my work.

I am a cognitive scientist seeking new professional opportunities. My primary interest lies in exploring how people interact with technology, particularly focusing on how different cognitive processes are involved, and how behavioral insights can be leveraged to develop more effective and user centered technology. While most of my academic projects have focused on language use in these interactions, my research interests extend beyond this area. Check out my portfolio to view the projects I have worked on.

If you would like to know more about me you can go to the about section.
