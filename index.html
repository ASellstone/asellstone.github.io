Welcome to Andreas Sellstone’s Website¶

This is my personal website where you can find information about me and my work, including my portfolio and blog.

Who am I? I am a cognitive scientist looking for work. I’m interested in the ways people interact with technology, particularly (but not exclusively) in how language is used in these interactions. There are a number of professions relating to cognition and technology that I am interested in pursuing, but as of right now I would especially like to pursue Technical Writing. However, I am almost equally interested in Human Factors Engineering, UX-Design/Research, and Data Analysis.

If you would like to know more about me you can go to the about section.
